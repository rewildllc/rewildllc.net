What We Do

Our Services

Habitat Management

Invasive species like privet and kudzu can make a landscape unusable for both wildlife and recreation. We use prescribed fire, mechanical removal, and targeted treatment to restore function to your land.

Native Plant Installations

We design and install native plantings scaled to your property and goals, from residential gardens to large conservation plantings that support pollinators, songbirds, and regional wildlife.

Ecology Research & Consulting

From wildlife population monitoring to habitat suitability assessments, we bring field research expertise to bear on real land management decisions.

Who We Are

Conservation-focused land stewardship

ReWild LLC is a Birmingham-based conservation land management company serving landowners across the Southeast. We work alongside our clients to improve their land for both wildlife and the people who use it, using applied ecology and adaptive management to get results.

But our work goes beyond removing invasives and installing native plants. We believe the best stewards are the ones who know their land intimately. They look forward to the arrival of the wood thrush every spring, notice when trilliums push up through the leaf litter, and know that deer need browse and cover to make it through the cold months. Aldo Leopold called this a land ethic: the idea that people are not separate from the land but part of it. We want to help our clients develop that relationship.
